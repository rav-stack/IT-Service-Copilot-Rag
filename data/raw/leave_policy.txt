LEAVE POLICY

1. Annual Leave:
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. Leave must be approved by the reporting manager in advance.

2. Sick Leave:
Employees can take up to 10 days of sick leave per year. A medical certificate is required for absences longer than 2 consecutive days.

3. Casual Leave:
Employees are eligible for 5 days of casual leave annually for personal matters or emergencies.

4. Carry Forward Policy:
Unused annual leave can be carried forward to the next year, up to a maximum of 10 days.

5. Leave Application Process:
All leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 3 days in advance (except emergencies).

6. Leave Without Pay:
If all leave balances are exhausted, employees may apply for leave without pay, subject to manager approval.
